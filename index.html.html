Welcome to the weather data!

This is a landing page from which you can access charts that summarize the relationship between a city's latitude and a weather pattern. Latitude reflects how close a city is to the north or south pole, with higher absolute values closer to the poles and zero representing the equator. Various weather patterns are influenced by how close a city is to the poles, while others are not. Weather data is pulled from the OpenWeatherMaps API.

Four weather patterns are viewed as a function of latitude: max temp, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. Max temp is related to latitude, while others seem to have a less strong relationship.
